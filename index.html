Enter the Maze

The Ultimate Maze Experience

LABYRAINTH is a heart-pounding sci-fi FPS that challenges you to navigate through procedurally generated alien labyrinths. Every playthrough is unique, every corner holds danger, and every second counts.

Combat System

Engage in intense firefights with alien creatures using an arsenal of upgradeable weapons. Each weapon has unique properties and upgrade paths.

Time-Based Survival

Race against the clock as the labyrinth changes around you. Environmental hazards increase as time runs out.

Power Abilities

Unlock and activate special powers to gain tactical advantages. From temporary invisibility to energy shields.

Resource Management

Collect rare materials to upgrade your equipment. Strategic resource allocation is key to survival.

Alien World

Explore beautifully crafted alien environments with dynamic lighting and atmospheric effects.

Procedural Generation

No two runs are the same. Experience infinite replayability with randomly generated maze layouts.
